Fun fact! I love airplanes and all kinds of aircraft. My favorites are from the jet fighter line of aircraft. These aircraft were made for air to air combat and other multirole purposes.

Each of these are broken into generations. These generations classify the techonoligical advances or jumps during the evolution of the jet fighter. There are currently five generations with the sixth on its way. I will not go into detail regarding the sixth generation since nothing is for sure besides who is developing them.

Additionally, I will provide some statistics and data for each aircraft in each generation. This will help visualize the which aircraft are better in which areas. Then I will also provide a table to show all of the aircraft that I listed and show how the advances over the years started to create gaps between performance for each generation and how much faster and more efficient these aircraft became in such a short amount of time.

The statistics shown will be the name of the aircraft, the size of the crew, lenght, wingspan, height, empty weight without fuel, maximum speed, range, and its rate of climb. The units of measure will be notated in the header of the columns.
